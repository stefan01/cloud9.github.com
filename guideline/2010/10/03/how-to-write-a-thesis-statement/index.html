What is a Thesis Statement? 03 Oct 2010

Thesis statement needs to answer a question about the issue you want to explore. Define your question, and answer it. It should be one- or two-sentence condensation of the argument.

A good thesis statement should include the following attributes:

  • take on a subject on which reasonable people could disagree
  • deal with a subject can be adequately treated
  • express one main idea
  • assert your conclusions about a subject
  • narrow the topic
  • Take a position on the topic
  • Use specific language
  • Make an assertion based on clearly stated support
  • Show that the topic is controversial to contrast your strong points
